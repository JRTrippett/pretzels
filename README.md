## 👋 Welcome to the Kitchen

If you're reading this, you've stumbled upon the blueprint for **Just Eatin Pretzels**. 

**Right now, there isn't any complicated code to download.** Instead, this page is the home base for our vision. We are building an app to solve a problem that every busy dad faces: *How do I eat well, pack on muscle, and be a great father without living in the kitchen or throwing away perfectly good groceries?*

## 📖 The Origin Story

This whole thing started as a conversation between two friends:
* **Jonathan:** A hands-on dad wanting to stay strong and set a good example for his family.
* **Andrew:** A full-time lawyer and father who works crazy hours and needs maximum efficiency.

Both of us were tired of the boring "tupperware chicken and rice" lifestyle. We realized the secret weapon to hitting our protein goals wasn't cooking *more*—it was cooking *smarter*. We started turning family dinner leftovers into high-protein lunches. It saved time, saved money, and fueled our workouts. 

Now, we're turning that exact system into an app.

## 💡 What We're Building

When **Just Eatin Pretzels** goes live, it will be the ultimate wingman for your fridge:

* 🥩 **The Leftover Engine:** Tell the app you have half a roast chicken, some tortillas, and an onion. It tells you how to make a 40g protein meal in 5 minutes.
* 💪 **No More Macro Math:** We do the nutritional heavy lifting so you just have to do the *actual* heavy lifting in the gym.
* ⏱️ **The 15-Minute Rule:** Between work and kids, you don't have two hours to prep. Because half the cooking is already done (thanks, last night's dinner), our recipes are blazingly fast.

## 🚧 Current Status: Building the Blueprint

*Is the app ready to download?* **Not quite yet.**

We are currently designing the layout, mapping out the leftover logic, and testing out the recipes (the fun part). We are using this space as our digital whiteboard to share our vision before we start writing the heavy code.
