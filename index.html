Built by Dads, For Dads

Just Eatin Pretzels started with a conversation between two friends: Jonathan and Andrew.


Andrew is a full-time lawyer. Jonathan is a busy dad. Both of them share the exact same struggle: wanting to eat well, stay in shape, pack on muscle, and be great fathers—without wasting food or spending all evening in the kitchen.


They realized that leftovers are the secret weapon to dad-gains. By repurposing family dinners into high-protein lunches, they solved the meal-prep puzzle. Now, they're sharing that system with you.
